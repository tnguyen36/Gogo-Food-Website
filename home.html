What is GOGO FOOD?

GOGO FOOD is an applicaion tool that lets customer order food at a restaurant ahead of time to avoid waiting in line and allow the food to be ready to be served as they arrive (Reservation).

Why is this important to us?

  • Positive social and economical impact for business and restaurants
  • Students can order food on the go using their mobile phone
  • The application will help college students save time when ordering food
  • An easy way to order/cancel food orders
  • The system will support multiple languages

How to get started (Website/App)

Mobile app

Follow the same procedures below for mobile app:

  1. Sign up for an account
  2. Join our reward program to get extra benefits for every purchases that you make
  3. Make an order request at your chosen restaurant and select the pick-up time
  4. Give payment information and confirm order
  5. Arrive at the selected restaurant and pick up your order at the designated time

That's it! Quick, Easy, and Reliable
